Victorian Secrets is an independent publisher dedicated to producing high-quality books from and about the nineteenth century, You can see feedback from some of our happy readers.
Our catalogue includes scholarly editions of unjustly neglected Victorian novels, along with biographies of nineteenth-century luminaries, such as Eugen Sandow, Jerome K. Jerome, Dame Clara Butt, and Thomas Bish.
To find out more or to buy our books, please take a look at our catalogue.
