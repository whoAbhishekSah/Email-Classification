Commonwealth Parliamentary Association UK Branch
CPA UK has a new website. To find out what we do, what we have coming up, and all the latest resources and materials please visit www.uk-cpa.org.
The Commonwealth Parliamentary Association UK Branch (CPA UK) provides opportunities for UK parliamentarians to liaise with fellow parliamentarians across the Commonwealth.
With a specific focus on parliamentary diplomacy and parliamentary strengthening activities, CPA UK seeks to foster co-operation and understanding between parliaments, promote good parliamentary practice and advance parliamentary democracy.
To find out more about CPA UK's programmes and how to participate, visit www.uk-cpa.org.

CPA UK's annual reports give an overview of our activites. For more information please visit www.uk-cpa.org
