HomeAbout usStampsMiniature SheetsFirst Day CoversMaxi CardsPostal StationeryPersonalitiesOlympic GamesCountriesFact SheetsResembling GamesWorld CupCancellationsCollectionsLinksContactWelcome to the website of Hockey on Stamps. Over 250 stamps featuring the sport of field hockey have been issued by 115 countries since the first hockey stamp was issued by Japan in 1951 on the occasion of the 6th National Athletic meeting at Hiroshima. This site includes stamps, miniature sheets, First Day Covers, Maxi and postcards, hockey cancellations and Philatelic items relating to field hockey. We are very grateful to Denys Shortt, a former England Hockey International for sponsoring this website.
